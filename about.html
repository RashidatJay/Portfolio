About me

Besides participating in tech programs to acquire technical and soft skills, i have gained a good level of understanding of HTML, CSS and Javascript from online courses on Udemy and Plural site.

As for writing, I have been a freelance writer for over two years and specialize in esports and technical articles. I have worked for several sites including Upcomer, Esportsgg and Blixgg. When i'm not coding or writing, i am mostly gaming or surfing the web.
